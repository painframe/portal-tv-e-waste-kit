# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
portal-tv-e-waste-kit project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation. We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind, patient, and welcoming — especially to people new to modding or to
  reviving old hardware.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, derogatory comments, or personal/political attacks.
- Trolling, deliberate intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and
will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they
deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful. Maintainers may remove,
edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are
not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (the repository, issues, pull
requests, and discussions) and when an individual is representing the project in
public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
to the project maintainers. For sensitive or confidential reports, use GitHub's
private reporting (the repository's **Security -> Report a vulnerability** form
accepts confidential messages) or contact a maintainer directly through the
[starbrightlab](https://github.com/starbrightlab) organization. For everything else,
open an issue. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly,
and maintainers will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
